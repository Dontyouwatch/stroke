Stroke Prediction Form

Enter your full name for identification.
Age is a key factor in stroke risk. Risk increases with age.
Men are at a slightly higher risk of stroke than women.
BMI helps assess obesity, a risk factor for stroke. Enter height (100-250 cm) and weight (30-200 kg).
Smoking damages blood vessels, increasing stroke risk.
Diabetes increases stroke risk by damaging blood vessels.
High blood pressure is the leading cause of stroke.
Atrial fibrillation increases stroke risk by causing blood clots.
A history of stroke significantly increases the risk of another stroke.
A family history of stroke may indicate a genetic predisposition.
